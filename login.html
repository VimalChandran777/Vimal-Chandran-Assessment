Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by British author J.K. Rowling. The novels revolve around the life of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his coming-of-age journey. The series has gained immense popularity worldwide and has been adapted into movies, theme parks, and merchandise. The fourth novel in the series, "Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire," is considered a pivotal moment in Harry's wizard training and coming-of-age journey .
